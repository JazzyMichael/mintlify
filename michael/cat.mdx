## This is Jazzy

Jazz is about understanding all the factors at play and deliberately breaking the common structures of music theory to explore sounds and experiences based on curiosity and exploration. I use this as a metaphor for my approach to life; as I strongly value curiosity, integrity, and taking risks. I will risk sounding awful for the potential to sound incredible.
